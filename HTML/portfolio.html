Achievements

Messi won his 7th Ballon d'Or(2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2015, 2019 and 2021) and set record for most Ballon d'Ors won.

Messi won his 1st major trophy with Argentina after defeating Brazil 1-0 in Copa America 2021.

Messi won his 2nd major trophy with Argentina after defeating Italy 3-0 in Finalissima 2022.

Messi won the FIFA World Cup after defeating France 3(2)-3(4) in Qatar World Cup 2022.

Messi won his 5th MOTM award in Qatar World Cup 2022 i.e. the highest number of MOTM awards won in that event. He also set record for the highest number of MOTM awards ever won i.e. 11 MOTM awards.

Messi won the Golden Ball award in Qatar World Cup 2022 and set record for the only player to receive the award twice(2014 and 2022).

Messi is the all time top scorer for Argentina i.e. 98 goals in 172 international appearances.
